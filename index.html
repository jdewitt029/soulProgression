About Me


Jordan DeWitt

I am an aspiring digital media artist currently enrolled in the Digital Media Art program at San Jose State University. I was born in Capitola, California but was raised in a small city called Tracy. My initial passion in life was baskebatall and it was not until I got out of high school that I had to find a new passion. I tried computer engineering, computer science, and then I stumbled into graphic design when I was at Delta College in Stockton. I was not really involved or interested in art up to this point in my life, but I loved the digital art courses that I was taking. After finally finding a new passion, I transferred to SJSU and enrolled in the DMA program which has completely opened up the doors of the relationship between technology and art for me. I do have a little bit of a coding background so Processing is one program that I find really fun to use. I hope to incorporate the new softwares and programs I am learning into unique works that have never been seen before.
