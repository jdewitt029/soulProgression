Soul Progression

Video created in Processing/Premiere, 1080x720, 2021



This project is a depiction of the development of a soul. A soul starts off pure and full of light, but as the world starts to show itself, the soul can start to be darkened. This crazy world can turn perfectly good souls into dark holes that some people never climb out of. I wanted to show that no matter how dark things may be or seem, that there is always light in that darkness. The light will ALWAYS prevail over the dark. Using Processing and Premiere, I created to a video convey that message.
